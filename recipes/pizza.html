Pizza

Description

A classic margherita pie, just like Nonna used to make. A light and fluffy crust, sweet tomatoes and bubbly mozzarella, garnished with fragrant basil. Tasty and timeless.


Ingredients

  • 9 ounces of Classic Pizza Dough
  • Flour
  • 3 ounces of crushed tomatoes
  • 3.5 ounces of fresh mozzarella
  • 1 teaspoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • About a handful of basil

Steps

  1. Heat oven up to 930 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Use flour to dust the work surface and the pizza peel
  3. Stretch the pizza dough ball out to 12 or 16 inches and lay it out on the pizza peel
  4. Top with sauce, followed by the mozzarella and olive oil (leave room for the crust)
  5. slide the pizza off the pizza peel and into your oven and cook for one to two minutes (rotate pizza every 20 seconds)
  6. Once cooked, remove the pizza from the oven and top with whole, fresh basil leaves

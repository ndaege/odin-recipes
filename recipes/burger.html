Cheeseburger

Description

Traditional grilled sandwich made with a juicy beef patty, topped with a slice of cheese. An American classic.


Ingredients

  • Ground chuck
  • Seasoning - salt and pepper
  • Cheese
  • Hamburger buns
  • Toppings - lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, etc.
  • Condiments - mayo, mustard, ketchup, etc.

Steps

  1. Mix chuck with seasoning
  2. Divide the meat into equal portions
  3. Press the meat with a plate, around one inch thick
  4. Heat over skillet on medium-high heat
  5. Cook burgers to preferrence
  6. Serve on toasted buns with choice of toppings and condiments
